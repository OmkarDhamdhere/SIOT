1. OVERVIEW

This IoT-based system monitors water parameters.

2.ABOUT DEVICE

Microcontroller Sensor Purpose Connection Type Model no
ESP32 pH Sensor Measures water acidity/alkalinity Analog (Model:ENB-C 201) ENB-C 201
TDS Sensor Measures total dissolved solids in water Analog
Temperature Sensor Monitors water temperature Digital DS18B20
Turbidity Sensor Measures water clarity Analog 20240611K

3.USER MANUAL

1. Connect 5v DC power suplly to Device by using Micro-USB cable.
2. Deep the Device in water sample.
3. Now the device is ready for use.
4. The results arer displayed in Prediction section

4.INSTRUCTIONS

1. The device should be operated only when it is fully submerged in water.
2. The model is designed to function optimally when submerged to a depth of up to 30 cm.
3. A DC power supply of up to 5 volts should be provided for proper operation.
